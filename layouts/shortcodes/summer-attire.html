Stay Cool During the Warm Months with Summer Attire

Starting in May, and running through September, we'll be relaxing the dress code for Stated Meetings and allowing "Summer Attire". Suits and ties are not required, but we still ask that you follow the general concept of "Business Casual," which may include:

  • Slacks
  • Chinos
  • Dress shirt
  • Polo shirt
  • Blazer
  • Sport coat
  • Dress shoes
  • Loafers

Bottom line: We want to keep it cool, clean, and professional!
