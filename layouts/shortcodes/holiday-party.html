Join us for Holiday Cheer at Our Annual Christmas Party!

Kick off the holiday season with a heart full of gratitude and joy at our annual Christmas Party! We'll be hosting the families and staff of our favorite charity, the RiteCare Childhood Language Center of Orange County.

We'll have gifts and activities for the children, treats, and a fantastic meal. There is no charge for dinner, but we encourage all our brothers to donate whatever amount you can to Language Center.

We'll wrap up the event with presents and a visit from Santa Claus himself!

Location and Information

The event is being held at the Anaheim Demolay Center, 303 West Lincoln Avenue, Anaheim, CA 92805. For questions and information, please contact Jamie Hopkins.

See you there!
